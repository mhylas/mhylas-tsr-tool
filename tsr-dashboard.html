Methodology & Data Notes
Click to expand
TSR Definition
TSR Formula
Cumulative TSR = (Adj. End Price ÷ Adj. Start Price) − 1
Annualized TSR = (1 + Cumulative TSR)1/years − 1
Adjusted price accounts for both stock splits and dividends reinvested, ensuring a true total shareholder return measure.
Price Averaging
Start price: 20-trading-day average ending on measurement start date
End price: closing price on measurement end date
Averaging the start price reduces the effect of short-term price volatility on the measurement baseline, consistent with best-practice compensation plan design.
Percentile Rank
Percentile rank is calculated among the disclosed peer group only (subject company excluded from denominator). A rank at the 50th percentile means the subject outperformed half the peer group.
Data Source
Price data sourced from Yahoo Finance or Financial Modeling Prep. Adjusted close prices reflect splits and dividends reinvested.
